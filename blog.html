5/27/22 - Week Zero

After contemplating my potential career path as it currently stands near the end of my first year of Community College, I purchased a Udemy course this week with the intention of completing as much of it as possible before the end of the Summer semester. In this effort, I will hopefully ascertain whether or not Web Development is a potential career for me and whether or not it is something that I might be interested in properly majoring in. I have been pleasantly surprised at how much the field interests me and in the little strides of progress that I have made since starting this week. I completed a basic HTML tutorial on freeCodeCamp.org and have just begun to break into the full content of the Udemy course that I am taking. I suppose time will tell whether it is something that I can keep up with alongside my regular college coursework, and whether it is to be a decisive pivot point in my education.
